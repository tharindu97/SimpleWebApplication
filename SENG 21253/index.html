Tourism History Culture Geography
Tourism is the largest industry in Nepal, and the largest source of foreign exchange and revenue. Possessing 8 of the 10 highest mountains in the world, Nepal is a hotspot destination for mountaineers, rock climbers and people seeking adventures. The history of Nepal is characterized by its isolated position in the Himalayas and its two neighbors, India and Tibet.Due to the arrival of disparate settler groups from outside through the ages, it is now a multi-ethnic, multi-cultural, multilingual country. A typical Nepalese meal is Dal bhat. Dal is a spicy lentil soup, served over bhat, served with tarkari together with achar or chutni. It consists of non-vegetarian as well as vegetarian items served with non-alcoholic beverages.
